Approach

Our method is simple: disciplined questions, authoritative sources, and elegant presentation. We keep government work and private engagements strictly separate; The House of Barton Wilson is an independent practice.

  • Discovery — understand goals, stakeholders, timelines.
  • Design — scope the archive, build a research plan, propose structure.
  • Development — execute research, draft prose, and iterate with editorial reviews.
  • Delivery — final manuscript or dossier, with citations and production‑ready files.
